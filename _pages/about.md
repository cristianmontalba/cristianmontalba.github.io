## Cristian Montalba

**Medical Technologist | MRI Researcher | AI & Neuroimaging Specialist**

I am an MRI Researcher with over 11 years of experience specialized in advanced neuroimaging protocols, quantitative biomarkers, and open science[cite: 1]. My work focuses on the intersection of medical imaging, data science, and clinical translation, developing machine learning pipelines to improve the characterization of complex neurological diseases[cite: 1].

I hold a Master's degree in Health Sciences Research from the Pontifical Catholic University of Chile[cite: 1] and have built a strong track record in both clinical management and international academic leadership[cite: 1].

---

### 🔬 Research Interests
* **Neuroimaging & Quantative Biomarkers:** Advanced acquisition and post-processing (fMRI, DTI, QSM)[cite: 1].
* **Machine Learning in Health:** Developing robust pipelines using *scikit-learn* and *PyRadiomics* for tumor and lesion characterization[cite: 1].
* **Open Science & Collaboration:** Designing reproducible imaging workflows and sequence prototyping using the *Pulseq* framework[cite: 1].
