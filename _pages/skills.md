**Core Expertise & Technical Stack**

*   **MRI & Pulse Sequence Design**: I coordinate clinical and research protocols across pediatric and adult populations with diverse neurological and psychiatric disorders. My work balances advanced 1.5T/3T image acquisition with open-source sequence development leveraging Pulseq. My hands-on expertise includes:
    *   **Acquisition & Paradigm Design**: Proficient in setting up clinical/research sequences, designing fMRI block-design visual and motor paradigms, and optimizing resting-state fMRI protocols.
    *   **Neuroimaging Processing**: Advanced workflow deployment for structural and functional modalities, specializing in **fMRI, DTI, and QSM** characterization using FSL and SPM.
  
*   **Machine Learning & Radiomics**: I build end-to-end data curation and feature engineering pipelines tailored to clinical and research datasets. My workflows focus on pattern recognition and biomarker extraction, primarily developed in Python and MATLAB. My core technical tools include:
    *   **Frameworks & Libraries**: Python (Scikit-Learn, Pandas, NumPy, basic PyTorch) and MATLAB.
    *   **Feature Extraction**: High-throughput radiomic profiling and biomarker discovery leveraging PyRadiomics.

**Global Leadership & Open Science**

*   **International Co-Leadership**: Co-Director of [MRI Together](https://mritogether.esmrmb.org/) and Co-Lead of SWiM / Conexxin under [CAMERA](https://www.cameramriafrica.org/) (ESMRMB). My efforts focus on:
    *   **Open Education & Advocacy**: Actively driving open-access MRI education and technical democratization across low- and middle-income countries (LMICs).
*   **Scientific Peer Review**: Invited reviewer evaluating cutting-edge translational research and clinical AI models.
    *   **Editorial Contributions**: Regular peer review for top-tier international journals, including *JMRI* (Journal of Magnetic Resonance Imaging), *Radiography*, and *Radiology: Artificial Intelligence*.
