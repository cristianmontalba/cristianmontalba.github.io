**Core Expertise & Technical Stack**

*   **MRI & Pulse Sequence Design**: I coordinate clinical and research protocols across pediatric and adult populations with diverse neurological and psychiatric disorders. My work balances advanced 1.5T/3T image acquisition with open-source sequence development leveraging Pulseq. My hands-on expertise includes:
    *   **Acquisition & Paradigm Design**: Proficient in setting up clinical/research sequences, designing fMRI block-design visual and motor paradigms, and optimizing resting-state fMRI protocols.
    *   **Neuroimaging Processing**: Advanced workflow deployment for structural and functional modalities, specializing in **fMRI, DTI, and QSM** using FSL and SPM.
  
*   **Machine Learning & Radiomics**: I build end-to-end data curation and feature engineering pipelines tailored to clinical and research datasets. My workflows focus on pattern recognition and biomarker extraction (using PyRadiomics), developed in Python (Scikit-Learn, Pandas, NumPy, PyTorch) and MATLAB.

**Global Leadership & Open Science**

*   **International Co-Leadership**: Co-Director of [MRI Together](https://mritogether.esmrmb.org/) and Co-Lead of SWiM / Conexxin under [CAMERA](https://www.cameramriafrica.org/) (ESMRMB), actively driving open-access MRI education and technical democratization across low- and middle-income countries (LMICs).
*   **Scientific Peer Review**: Invited reviewer evaluating cutting-edge translational research for top-tier journals, including *JMRI* (Journal of Magnetic Resonance Imaging), *Radiography*, and *Radiology: Artificial Intelligence*.
