**Core Expertise & Technical Stack**

*   **MRI & Pulse Sequence Design**: Comprehensive clinical and research protocol management across pediatric and adult cohorts. Expertise in advanced 1.5T/3T image acquisition, fMRI paradigm design, and structural/functional neuroimaging processing using FSL and SPM. Proficient in open-source sequence development leveraging Pulseq.
*   **Machine Learning & Radiomics**: End-to-end data curation, high-throughput feature engineering, and pattern recognition tailored to clinical datasets. Advanced pipelines developed in Python (Scikit-Learn, Pandas, NumPy, basic PyTorch) and MATLAB, with specialized expertise in biomarker extraction using PyRadiomics.

**Global Leadership & Open Science**

*   **International Co-Leadership**: Co-Director of [MRI Together](https://mritogether.esmrmb.org/) and Co-Lead of SWiM / Conexxin under [CAMERA](https://www.cameramriafrica.org/) (ESMRMB), actively driving open-access MRI education and technical democratization across low- and middle-income countries (LMICs).
*   **Scientific Peer Review**: Invited reviewer evaluating cutting-edge translational research for top-tier journals, including *JMRI* (Journal of Magnetic Resonance Imaging), *Radiography*, and *Radiology: Artificial Intelligence*.
